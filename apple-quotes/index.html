    About

    Why a constellation of words?

    Words shape worlds.

    For more than four decades, the people at Apple have defined what a computer, a phone, a piece of music, a watch — and a culture — can be. The quotes below are the small, durable moments behind those inventions.

    Built with care.

    AppleQuotes is a single-page experience, designed in the spirit of the very words it collects. The point cloud is rendered in real time, every frame, using only the browser's canvas.

    Open & evolving.

    Add new voices, new quotes, new languages. The data is plain JSON. Visit the GitHub repository to contribute.
